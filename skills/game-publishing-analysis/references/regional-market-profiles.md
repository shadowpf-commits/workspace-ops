# Regional Market Profiles — SEA & LATAM

## Overview

This reference contains standardized market profiles for key game publishing markets in Southeast Asia (SEA) and Latin America (LATAM). Use these profiles when building go-to-market strategies.

---

## SEA Markets

---

### 🇻🇳 Vietnam (VN)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: ~100M
- Gaming market: $1.2B+ (fastest growing in SEA)
- Active gamers: 55M+ (over half the population)
- 96% prefer mobile gaming

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-35, predominantly male
- Play habits: Mobile-first, short session lengths, social/play with friends
- Payment: Low ARPU but growing; prefer in-app purchases over cards
- Top games: Garena Free Fire, League of Legends: Wild Rift, Lien Quan Mobile

**Platform Distribution**
- Mobile: ~80%
- PC/Console: ~20%
- App stores: Google Play dominant, App Store for iOS

**Channel Performance (Empirical)**
| Channel | Viability | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Google UAC | ✅ Excellent | 70%+ of spend, best efficiency |
| Facebook | ❌ Poor | CPR often 3x+ higher than Google |
| TikTok | ⚠️ Moderate | Works with KOL boost; pure paid underperforms |
| Apple Search Ads | ✅ Good | High intent, best R7 |

**Cultural Notes**
- Highly nationalistic; respond to local language + Vietnam-specific content
- Family/collectivist values resonate in storytelling
- Tet (Vietnamese New Year, Jan/Feb) is key seasonal moment
- Esports passion is high — Vietnam teams in regional esports are popular

**Marketing Calendar**
- Jan/Feb: Tet (Vietnamese New Year) — biggest holiday
- June-Aug: Summer vacation — student gamers more active
- Sept: National Day (2 Sept)
- No major religious marketing constraints

**Localize Requirements**
- Language: Vietnamese (not Thai, not Khmer)
- Tone: Warm, family-oriented, local humor
- Do NOT: Use generic Asian imagery; treat as Thai/Khmer market
- Subtitle translations must be native-speaker quality

### 🇮🇩 Indonesia (ID)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 280M+
- Gaming market: Large and growing
- Smartphone adoption: 99.4% among internet users
- 96% of gamers prefer mobile titles

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-35, predominantly male
- Play habits: Mobile-first, social gaming, competitive titles (Mobile Legends is king)
- Payment: Low-medium ARPU; mobile carrier billing still relevant in some segments
- Top games: Mobile Legends Bang Bang (MLBB), Free Fire, PUBG Mobile

**Platform Distribution**
- Mobile: ~90%+
- PC/Console: ~10%
- Google Play dominant

**Channel Performance (Empirical)**
| Channel | Viability | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Google UAC | ✅ Excellent | 99%+ of spend, dominant |
| Facebook | ❌ Poor | Minimal traction |
| TikTok | ⚠️ Growing | Rising platform, good with localization |
| Apple Search Ads | ✅ Good | High intent |

**Cultural Notes**
- 84% Muslim — religious sensitivity matters for content
- Family-oriented, community values
- Mobile Legends is not just a game — it's a social platform
- Indonesian is NOT Malay — separate languages with significant differences
- Local cultural elements: Jakarta, Java/Javanese culture dominates pop culture

**Marketing Calendar**
- Feb-Apr: Ramadan (斋月) + Eid al-Fitr (开斋节/Lebaran) — HUGE opportunity for Muslim audience
- Aug 17: Indonesian Independence Day — patriotic themes work
- June-Aug: School holidays — youth engagement peaks
- Avoid: Pork-related content, alcohol promotion, non-halal imagery

**Localize Requirements**
- Language: Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia) — NOT Bahasa Malaysia
- Tone: Community-focused, warm, family values
- Imagery: Modest dress standards for characters
- Music: Local instruments/styles preferred
- Do NOT: Use Malaysian-language content; treat as separate market

### 🇵🇭 Philippines (PH)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 115M+
- Gaming market: Growing rapidly
- Mobile gaming: Dominant platform

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-35, balanced gender ratio
- Play habits: Mobile-first, social gaming, love competitive titles
- Payment: Medium ARPU; GCash Maya growing for digital payments
- Top games: Mobile Legends, League of Legends: Wild Rift, Genshin Impact

**Cultural Notes**
- Strong US cultural influence (English widely spoken)
- Community and family oriented
- Gaming is social — guilds, teams, communities matter
- TikTok usage growing rapidly

**Marketing Calendar**
- June: Independence Day (12 June) — patriotic content works
- December: Christmas season — extended family gatherings
- March-April: Holy Week (Semana Santa)

### 🇲🇾 Malaysia (MY)

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-40, tech-savvy
- Play habits: Mobile + PC hybrid; esports viewership high
- Payment: Higher ARPU than regional neighbors
- Top games: Mobile Legends, PUBG Mobile, Valorant

**Cultural Notes**
- Multi-ethnic: Malay (Muslim), Chinese, Indian communities
- Each ethnic group has distinct gaming preferences
- Bahasa Malaysia is official but English widely used in gaming
- Malay Muslims = similar sensitivity to Indonesia

**Marketing Calendar**
- Hari Raya Aidilfitri (Eid al-Fitr) — major holiday
- Chinese New Year — for Chinese-Malaysian community
- Deepavali — for Indian-Malaysian community

## LATAM Markets

---

### 🇲🇽 Mexico (MX)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 130M+
- Gaming market: $2B+
- Largest gaming market in LATAM
- 2026: Co-host of FIFA World Cup — massive marketing opportunity

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-40, predominantly male
- Play habits: Mobile + Console; console historically strong but mobile growing
- Payment: Medium ARPU; credit card, OXXO cash payments relevant
- Top games: Free Fire, League of Legends, Valorant, Minecraft

**Platform Distribution**
- Mobile: ~55%
- Console: ~30%
- PC: ~15%

**Channel Performance (Empirical)**
| Channel | Viability | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Google UAC | ✅ Excellent | ~99%+ of spend, best efficiency |
| Facebook | ⚠️ Limited | Minimal paid performance |
| TikTok | ⚠️ Limited | Not tested extensively; YouTube preferred |
| YouTube | ✅ Strong | Key platform for game discovery |
| Apple Search Ads | ✅ Good | High intent |

**Cultural Notes**
- Spanish-speaking — LATAM Spanish is NOT Spain Spanish
- Mexican Spanish has distinct slang, idioms, and pop culture references
- Football/soccer is religion — national team games = mass engagement peaks
- Family-oriented, community gatherings (fiestas) are cultural core
- US cultural influence strong (Hollywood, music)

**World Cup 2026 Context**
- Mexico is co-host with USA and Canada
- Games will be in Mexico City, Guadalajara, Monterrey
- Expected to be MASSIVE national moment
- June-July 2026: All marketing should tie into World Cup
- Mexican fan culture is passionate, often diaspora-driven (USMEX fans)

**Marketing Calendar**
- July: World Cup (if Mexico playing) — absolute peak
- Sept 16: Mexican Independence Day — "Grito de Dolores"
- Nov 20: Mexican Revolution Day
- Dec 12: Day of the Virgin of Guadalupe
- April: Easter/Spring Break (Semana Santa)

**Localize Requirements**
- Language: Mexican Spanish (NOT standard/castilian)
- Tone: Warm, passionate, community/family-oriented
- Humor: Mexican slang and references
- Do NOT: Use Spain Spanish; Mexican audience will notice

### 🇦🇷 Argentina (AR)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 46M+
- Gaming market: Significant, growing
- Independent server required (not merged with MX)

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-40, predominantly male
- Play habits: Mobile + PC; Steam is popular for PC gaming
- Payment: Dollarized economy (inflation) = unique pricing sensitivity
- Top games: FIFA, League of Legends, Counter-Strike, Free Fire

**Cultural Notes**
- Argentine Spanish has VERY distinct slang ("vos" instead of "tú", unique idioms)
- Football (soccer) is a DEEP religion — Messi is almost god-like
- Tango, asado (barbecue), mate culture are national identity
- July 9: Independence Day — patriotic themes resonate
- Argentina has strong independent game dev community

**Server Consideration**
- Argentina operates on independent server infrastructure
- Must be treated as separate launch from day one
- User matching may be regional (AR + CL + UY = some combination)

**Marketing Calendar**
- June-July: Winter vacation — indoor gaming increases
- July 9: Independence Day — patriotic marketing works
- June-August: School winter break (students = more gaming time)
- World Cup 2026: Argentina are defending champions — if they advance, MASSIVE peaks

**Localize Requirements**
- Language: Argentine Spanish ("voseo", unique slang)
- Tone: Passionate, proud, slightly self-deprecating humor
- Football references: Use Lionel Messi, Argentine national team carefully
- Do NOT: Confuse with Mexican Spanish — very different

### 🇧🇷 Brazil (BR)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 215M+
- Gaming market: $2.5B+ (largest in LATAM)
- Independent server required (not merged with MX/AR)

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-40, balanced gender
- Play habits: Mobile-first; TikTok usage is very high
- Payment: Boleto, PIX, credit card; unique payment landscape
- Top games: Free Fire (massive), League of Legends, Minecraft, FIFA

**Platform Distribution**
- Mobile: ~65%
- Console: ~25%
- PC: ~10%

**Channel Performance (Empirical)**
| Channel | Viability | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Google UAC | ✅ Excellent | Primary channel |
| TikTok | ✅ Strong | BR is one of TikTok's largest gaming markets globally |
| YouTube | ✅ Strong | Game discovery and content consumption |
| Facebook | ⚠️ Limited | Not dominant |
| Apple Search Ads | ✅ Good | High intent |

**Cultural Notes**
- Portuguese-speaking — Brazilian Portuguese is NOT European Portuguese
- Football/soccer is national religion — World Cup =全民疯狂
- Samba, carnival, beach culture = energetic, vibrant brand associations
- Community/team play is important
- Gamers are price-conscious but growing paying segment

**Server Consideration**
- Brazil operates on independent server
- Portuguese language required (NOT Spanish)
- LATAM Spanish-speaking countries do NOT cover Brazil

**Marketing Calendar**
- Feb: Carnival (pre-Lenten) — massive cultural moment
- June-August: Winter vacation (South hemisphere)
- Sept 7: Independence Day
- July: Winter break + potential World Cup overlap
- June: Festa Junina (traditional Northeast Brazil festival)

**Localize Requirements**
- Language: Brazilian Portuguese (not European Portuguese)
- Tone: Energetic, warm, community-oriented
- Humor: Brazilian style (different from other LATAM)
- Carnival/Football themes resonate strongly
- Do NOT: Use Spanish content; treat as separate language market
