# CN / TW / HK Market Profile

## Overview

Greater China is a complex, fragmented market requiring separate go-to-market strategies for each region. Do NOT treat CN, TW, and HK as interchangeable.

---

## ⚠️ Empirical Finding: MOBA Users Are Primary Target

**Based on prior UA tests across multiple markets, MOBA category users consistently show the BEST performance metrics (D7, R2, CPR) compared to other audience segments.**

Therefore:
- **Core TA (MOBA players) = PRIMARY acquisition target**, not a secondary consideration
- FPS/TPS fans are complementary audiences, not the priority
- Reference game audiences (Honor of Kings, Mobile Legends) are the prime lookalike seeds for UA in CN/TW/HK markets

## 🇨🇳 China Mainland (CN)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 1.4B+
- Gaming market: $45B+ (largest in world)
- Mobile gaming: ~75% of total gaming revenue
- Regulatory environment: Strict — game licensing (版号) required for all commercial games

**Target Audience Sizing (MOBA-Shooter Type)**
- Core TA (active MOBA players): ~80-120M
- Secondary TA (FPS/TPS fans): ~60-80M
- Total Addressable (mobile action players): ~200-280M
- Reachable TA (if 版号 obtained + local partner): ~30-60M
- TA Profile: Male 16-40; Honor of Kings + Wild Rift are the MOBA baseline; competitive + social gaming is mainstream
- MOBA crossover rate (Honor of Kings → MOBA-shooter): Estimated 30-40%
- ⚠️ Gate: Requires 版号 + local publishing partner. Without CN entity, this market is inaccessible.

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-45, balanced gender in mid-core/casual
- Play habits: Mobile-first, long sessions, social/guild features critical
- Payment: WeChat Pay, Alipay dominant; high ARPU for core payers
- Top games: Honor of Kings, League of Legends: Wild Rift, Genshin Impact, Subway Surfers

**Platform Distribution**
- Android: ~70% (via third-party app stores: Tencent, 华为, OPPO, Vivo, Xiaomi)
- iOS: ~30%
- PC: Still significant for mid-core/hardcore

**Channel Performance**
| Channel | Viability | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| 腾讯广告 (Tencent Ads) | ✅ Essential | WeChat, QQ, Tencent ecosystem; requires CN business entity |
| 字节跳动 (ByteDance) | ✅ Strong | 抖音/TikTok, 今日头条; requires CN entity |
| 华为广告 (Huawei Ads) | ✅ Growing | Huawei app store dominance; bypass Google |
| 百度推广 (Baidu) | ⚠️ Moderate | Search-based; less effective for gaming |
| 快手 (Kuaishou) | ✅ Strong | Short video; good for casual/mid-core |
| Apple Search Ads | ✅ Good | iOS users have higher LTV |

**Regulatory Considerations**
- All games need 版号 (game license) — process takes 3-12+ months
- Content review required for all in-app purchases
- Minor protection regulations (anti-addiction for under-18)
- Real-name registration required
- Foreign games face additional scrutiny; local partner often required

**Cultural Notes**
- CN players are highly receptive to domestic-themed content (古风, 武侠, 三国)
- But also respond to global high-quality IPs (Genshin proves this)
- Social features + guild features are survival requirements, not nice-to-haves
- Mobile esports is massive; Wild Rift and Honor of Kings have huge viewership

**Marketing Calendar**
| Time | Event | Marketing Value | Action |
|------|-------|----------------|--------|
| Jan-Feb | Chinese New Year (春节) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Largest spending period; red packets, login bonuses |
| May | Labor Day黄金周 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 5-day holiday; gaming time up |
| July-Aug | Summer vacation | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Student gamers active |
| Oct 1-7 | National Day黄金周 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Second biggest holiday |
| Nov 11 | Singles Day (双十一) | ⭐⭐⭐ | Shopping festival; can tie in with bundles |
| Dec-Jan | Year-end | ⭐⭐⭐ | Winter events, New Year |

**Localization Requirements**
- Simplified Chinese (简体中文) — NOT Traditional
- CN-specific regulatory compliance
- Local payment integration (WeChat Pay, Alipay)
- Server must be CN-based (data localization laws)
- Local customer service required

**CN Go-to-Market Path**
1. Find local publishing partner (腾讯, 网易, or specialist publisher)
2. Obtain 版号 (takes months — start early)
3. Adapt content to CN regulatory standards
4. Integrate local payment and anti-addiction systems
5. Plan launch around CN holiday calendar

## 🇹🇼 Taiwan (TW)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 23M+
- Gaming market: ~$1.5B
- Mobile gaming: Dominant platform
- No regulatory blocking for foreign games

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-40, predominantly male
- Play habits: Mobile-first; RPG and mid-core popular
- Payment: High ARPU; credit card + mobile carrier billing
- Top games: Mobile Legends, Genshin Impact, LINE Rangers, Free Fire

**Target Audience Sizing (MOBA-Shooter Type)**
- Core TA (active MOBA players): ~2-3M
- Secondary TA (FPS/TPS fans): ~1.5-2.5M
- Total Addressable (mobile action players): ~4-6M
- Reachable TA (with budget/media access): ~1.5-3M
- TA Profile: Male 18-35; Mobile Legends is the dominant MOBA; no Honor of Kings presence; higher ARPU than SEA
- MOBA crossover rate (ML → MOBA-shooter): Estimated 35-45%

**Platform Distribution**
- Mobile: ~65%
- PC: ~25%
- Console: ~10%
- Google Play + iOS dominant; no major local Android store

**Channel Performance**
| Channel | Viability | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Google UAC | ✅ Excellent | Primary channel for most games |
| Facebook/Meta | ✅ Strong | TW has high FB usage |
| TikTok | ✅ Growing | Younger audience |
| YouTube | ✅ Strong | Gaming content discovery |
| Apple Search Ads | ✅ Good | High intent |

**Cultural Notes**
- Traditional Chinese characters (正體中文) — do NOT use Simplified
- Taiwan players are price-sensitive but generous with in-app purchases
- Mobile Legends is THE dominant MOBA (not Honor of Kings)
- Anime-style games perform well
- Community/team features important

**Marketing Calendar**
| Time | Event | Marketing Value | Action |
|------|-------|----------------|--------|
| Jan-Feb | Chinese New Year | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Red envelopes, holiday events |
| Feb | Lantern Festival | ⭐⭐⭐ | Traditional event; can tie to games |
| Oct 10 | Double Ten Day (國慶日) | ⭐⭐⭐ | Patriotic content |
| Dec 25 | Christmas | ⭐⭐⭐ | Holiday themes |
| Summer | Summer vacation | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Students active |

**Localization Requirements**
- Traditional Chinese (正體中文) — native quality
- Taiwan-specific slang and expressions
- Local KOL/streamer integration is critical (twitch, YouTube)
- Gamers in TW follow mainland CN trends closely

## 🇭🇰 Hong Kong (HK)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 7.5M+
- Gaming market: ~$500M
- Highly mobile-first; affluent user base

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-40, balanced gender
- Play habits: Mobile-first; English fluency relatively high
- Payment: High ARPU; credit card, Octopus (local e-payment)
- Top games: Mobile Legends, Free Fire, Genshin Impact

**Target Audience Sizing (MOBA-Shooter Type)**
- Core TA (active MOBA players): ~0.6-1M
- Secondary TA (FPS/TPS fans): ~0.4-0.8M
- Total Addressable (mobile action players): ~1.2-2M
- Reachable TA (with budget/media access): ~0.5-1M
- TA Profile: Affluent, bilingual; MLBB is gateway; higher per-capita spending than CN/TW
- MOBA crossover rate: Estimated 30-40%

**Platform Distribution**
- Mobile: ~70%
- PC/Console: ~30%
- Google Play + iOS dominant

**Channel Performance**
| Channel | Viability | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Google UAC | ✅ Excellent | Primary channel |
| Facebook/Meta | ✅ Strong | High social media usage |
| TikTok | ✅ Growing | Younger demographic |
| Apple Search Ads | ✅ Good | Affluent iOS users |

**Cultural Notes**
- Bilingual: Traditional Chinese + English
- Gaming culture influenced by both CN and Western markets
- Mobile Legends is popular (gateway to SEA)
- Relatively fast adoption of new trends
- HK players have higher average spending than CN/TW

**Marketing Calendar**
| Time | Event | Marketing Value | Action |
|------|-------|----------------|--------|
| Jan-Feb | Chinese New Year | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Major spending period |
| July 1 | HK Establishment Day | ⭐⭐⭐ | Patriotic local content |
| Oct 1 | National Day | ⭐⭐⭐ | Mixed CN/HK sentiment |
| Dec 25-26 | Christmas | ⭐⭐⭐ | Holiday events |

**Localization Requirements**
- Traditional Chinese (正體中文) or bilingual (CN/EN)
- No regulatory barriers for foreign games
- Localized customer service in Cantonese preferred
