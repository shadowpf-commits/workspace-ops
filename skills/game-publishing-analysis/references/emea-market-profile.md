# EMEA Market Profile — Europe & Middle East

## Overview

EMEA (Europe, Middle East, Africa) is a diverse region with vastly different market maturity, platform preferences, and cultural contexts. This profile covers key European markets (DE, FR, ES, IT, UK, TR) and Middle East (Turkey, UAE, Saudi Arabia).

---

## ⚠️ Empirical Finding: MOBA Users Are Primary Target

**Based on prior UA tests, MOBA category users (LoL, Wild Rift, Clash Royale audiences) consistently outperform other segments in Europe.**

Therefore:
- **Core TA (MOBA players) = PRIMARY acquisition target** in DE, FR, UK, ES, IT
- **Note on MENA (TR/UAE/SA):** MOBA culture is less dominant — FPS (PUBG, Free Fire) is the primary genre in TR and SA; adjust targeting accordingly
- Reference game audiences (LoL, Clash Royale) are the prime lookalike seeds for UA in European markets

## 🇩🇪 Germany (DE)

**Market Size & Growth**
- Population: 84M+
- Gaming market: $6B+
- Mobile gaming: Growing rapidly; PC/console historically strong

**User Profile**
- Age: 18-45, balanced gender
- Play habits: Mobile + PC; mid-core games popular
- Payment: High ARPU; SEPA direct debit, credit card, PayPal
- Top games: FIFA, League of Legends, World of Tanks, Candy Crush

**Target Audience Sizing (MOBA-Shooter Type)**
- Core TA (active MOBA players): ~5-8M
- Secondary TA (FPS/TPS fans): ~4-7M
- Total Addressable (mobile action players): ~12-18M
- Reachable TA (with budget/media access): ~3-8M
- TA Profile: Male 16-40; LoL is the MOBA reference; German esports scene is mature
- MOBA crossover rate: Estimated 25-35%

**Platform Distribution**
- Mobile: ~45%
- PC: ~30%
- Console: ~25%
- Google Play + iOS

**Channel Performance**
| Channel | Viability | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Google UAC | ✅ Excellent | Primary performance channel |
| Apple Search Ads | ✅ Strong | High LTV, good for mid-core |
| Facebook/Meta | ✅ Good | Works in DE; German audience is active |
| TikTok | ✅ Strong | 18-34 demographic strong |
| YouTube | ✅ Excellent | Gaming content discovery |
| Unity Ads | ✅ Good | Works for mid-core/casual |

**Cultural Notes**
- German players value fairness and transparency
- Quality expectations high; bugs/unpolished content gets called out
- German language is essential for casual/mid-core (not everyone speaks English)
- Esports viewership is high (League of Legends, Counter-Strike)
