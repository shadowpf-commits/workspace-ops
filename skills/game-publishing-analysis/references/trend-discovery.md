# Trend Discovery Methodology

## Overview

Finding marketing moments is NOT just about holidays. The best opportunities come from:
- **Seasonal behavior shifts** (vacations, weather changes)
- **Cultural rhythms** (regional events, school calendars)
- **Pop culture moments** (viral trends, local obsessions)
- **Sports/Esports calendars** (not just World Cup)
- **Platform dynamics** (when do users go online?)

This methodology helps systematically discover exploitable trends by region.

---

## Core Framework: The TREND Filter

For each market, scan these 5 dimensions:

### T — Temporal (时间节点)
- School/university calendars
- National work schedules
- Paycheck cycles (when do people have money?)
- Religious calendars (Ramadan, Lent, etc.)

### R — Regional Events (区域事件)
- National holidays
- Cultural festivals unique to the region
- Local sports events (not just international)
- Political/civic moments

### E — Entertainment Cycles (娱乐周期)
- TV show seasons (when do people stay home?)
- Movie release windows
- Music festival seasons
- Gaming event calendars (E3, Gamescom, local events)

### N — Notable Trends (流行趋势)
- Local social media obsessions
- Viral challenges specific to that country
- Influencer moments
- Emerging local memes/slang

### D — Digital Behavior (数字行为)
- Platform usage patterns (when is TikTok most active?)
- Gaming session time distributions
- Weekend vs weekday differences
- Back-to-school vs summer engagement

## Regional Trend Calendars

### 🇻🇳 Vietnam (VN)

**School Calendar:**
- Summer vacation: June 1 - August 31 (major! students home all day)
- Tet holiday: January (Lunar New Year, 1-2 weeks off)
- Mid-year break: May (short)
- Back to school: September 5

**Key Trend Windows:**
| Time | Trend | Marketing Value | Action |
|------|--------|---------------|--------|
| June-August | Summer vacation | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Students home, gaming time spikes, casual content works |
| January-February | Tet | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Family gatherings, gift-giving, return home |
| September | Back to school | ⭐⭐ | School-year start, can push "study breaks" |
| November-December | Year-end | ⭐⭐⭐ | Winter break prep, indoor gaming increase |

**Digital Behavior:**
- TikTok peak hours: 7-10 PM (after dinner)
- Weekend gaming spikes: Saturday afternoon
- School holidays: ALL DAY gaming on weekdays

**Local Cultural Moments:**
- Football (V-League) has local following
- Garena Free Fire dominates VN esports
- Mobile Legends is huge
- Zing MP3 is culturally important (music matters in games)

## Trend Discovery Process

When analyzing a market for trend opportunities:

### Step 1: Map the Calendar
For the target month, list:
- [ ] School/university schedule
- [ ] National holidays
- [ ] Religious events
- [ ] Cultural festivals
- [ ] Sports events
- [ ] Entertainment releases

### Step 2: Identify Behavior Shifts
Ask:
- When do people have MORE time to game?
- When do people have LESS time?
- When is mood celebratory (spending mood)?
- When is mood reflective (stay-home mood)?

### Step 3: Find Platform-Specific Moments
Ask:
- What are local influencers talking about?
- What challenges/hashtags are trending?
- What local events are getting social media attention?

### Step 4: Look for Cross-Cultural Bridges
Ask:
- What global trends can be localized?
- What local events have international appeal?
- How can game mechanics tie into real-world moments?

## Trend + Game Mechanics Mapping

Not every trend needs a full campaign. Categorize:

### Tier 1: Full Campaign
- Major sporting event (World Cup)
- Major cultural moment (Carnival, Tet)
- Product-launch-worthy

### Tier 2: In-Game Event
- Seasonal login bonus
- Limited-time skin
- Themed event mode
- Bracket/prediction mini-game

### Tier 3: Content Only
- Social media post
- Email/newsletter
- KOL talking points
- Blog content
